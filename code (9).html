What is Mental Health?

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. Good mental health helps us handle stress, relate to others, and make choices. According to the World Health Organization (WHO), one in four people will experience a mental health issue at some point in their lives.

Signs of Mental Health Challenges

  • Feeling persistently sad or anxious
  • Changes in sleep or appetite
  • Difficulty concentrating or making decisions
  • Withdrawal from social activities
  • Thoughts of self-harm

Useful Tips for Better Mental Health

  • Stay connected with loved ones
  • Exercise regularly and eat a balanced diet
  • Practice mindfulness or meditation
  • Seek professional help if needed—it's a sign of strength
  • Limit screen time and prioritize rest

Resources

If you're struggling, reach out to:

  • WHO Mental Health Resources
  • Mental Health America
  • Hotlines: In the US, call 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline)
